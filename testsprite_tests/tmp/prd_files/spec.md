## Detailed Solution Area Analysis
### "#" Audit Management
**Strategic Purpose**: Provides comprehensive audit management capabilities from planning through remediation tracking.
**Key Business Capabilities**:
- **Issues Management**: Centralizes audit findings, recommendations, and corrective actions with automated workflows for assignment, tracking, and closure
- **Audit Engagements & Workpapers**: Manages the complete audit execution process including workpaper management, evidence collection, and review workflows
- **Audit Planning & Quality**: Enables risk-based audit planning, resource allocation, and quality assurance processes
**Integration Points**: Risk management, compliance management, controls assurance
**Maturity Progression**: Basic finding tracking → Full audit lifecycle → Advanced audit analytics
### "#" ESG Management
**Strategic Purpose**: Comprehensive Environmental, Social, and Governance program management aligned with emerging regulatory requirements.
**Key Business Capabilities**:
- **ESG Management**: Framework for managing ESG initiatives, metrics, and reporting across the enterprise
- **Carbon Management**: Carbon footprint measurement, reduction planning, and sustainability reporting
- **ESG Disclosure Management**: Automated ESG reporting for regulatory compliance and stakeholder communication
- **ESG Portfolio Management**: Investment portfolio ESG risk assessment and sustainable investment guidance
- **Double Materiality Calculator**: Assessment tool for identifying material ESG topics from impact and financial perspectives
**Integration Points**: Risk management, regulatory compliance, data governance
**Maturity Progression**: Basic ESG tracking → Comprehensive ESG program → Advanced ESG analytics and AI
### "#" IT & Security Risk Management
**Strategic Purpose**: Holistic IT and cybersecurity risk management across technology infrastructure and operations.
**Key Business Capabilities**:
- **Cyber Incident & Breach Response**: End-to-end incident response workflow from detection through lessons learned
- **IT Security Policy Program Management**: Policy lifecycle management specific to IT and security domains
- **IT Security Vulnerabilities Program**: Vulnerability assessment, prioritization, and remediation tracking
- **IT Regulatory Management**: Technology-specific regulatory compliance (SOX IT controls, privacy regulations)
- **IT Risk Management**: Technology risk assessment and mitigation planning
- **IT Controls Assurance**: Automated testing and validation of IT controls
- **PCI Management**: Payment Card Industry compliance program management
- **ISMS**: ISO 27001 Information Security Management System implementation
- **CMMC Management**: Defense contractor cybersecurity compliance
**Integration Points**: Enterprise risk management, compliance, third-party risk
**Maturity Progression**: Basic IT risk tracking → Comprehensive security program → Advanced threat intelligence integration
### "#" Enterprise & Operational Risk Management
**Strategic Purpose**: Enterprise-wide risk management from strategic risks to day-to-day operational risks.
**Key Business Capabilities**:
- **Risk Catalog**: Standardized enterprise risk taxonomy and classification system
- **Enterprise Risk Management**: Strategic risk identification, assessment, and reporting to senior management
- **Risk Assessment Management**: Standardized risk assessment methodologies and workflows
- **Loss Event Management**: Operational loss tracking, analysis, and trending
- **Key Indicator Management**: KRI/KPI monitoring with automated alerting and escalation
- **Operational Risk Management**: Business process risk management and control monitoring
**Integration Points**: All other solution areas as the foundational risk platform
**Maturity Progression**: Basic risk registers → Integrated risk management → Predictive risk analytics
### "#" Regulatory & Corporate Compliance Management
**Strategic Purpose**: Comprehensive regulatory and corporate compliance management across all business functions.
**Key Business Capabilities**:
- **AI Governance**: Artificial intelligence ethics, bias monitoring, and regulatory compliance
- **Data Governance**: Data quality, privacy, and protection program management
- **Privacy Program Management**: GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulation compliance
- **Policy Program Management**: Enterprise policy creation, distribution, acknowledgment, and enforcement
- **Corporate Obligations Management**: Regulatory requirement identification, mapping, and monitoring
- **Controls Assurance Program Management**: Internal controls testing and validation across all domains
- **Financial Controls Monitoring**: SOX compliance and financial reporting controls oversight
**Integration Points**: Risk management, audit management, data governance
**Maturity Progression**: Reactive compliance → Proactive compliance management → AI-driven compliance optimization
### "#" Resilience Management
**Strategic Purpose**: Organizational resilience and business continuity across all types of disruptions.
**Key Business Capabilities**:
- **Business Impact Analysis**: Systematic assessment of business process criticality and recovery requirements
- **Incident Management**: Business disruption response and coordination
- **Business Continuity & IT Disaster Recovery Planning**: Comprehensive continuity and recovery planning
- **Crisis Management**: Crisis response coordination and stakeholder communication
- **Operational Scenario Analysis**: Risk scenario modeling and stress testing
**Integration Points**: Risk management, IT security, third-party risk
**Maturity Progression**: Basic BC plans → Integrated resilience program → Advanced scenario modeling
### "#" Third Party Risk Management
**Strategic Purpose**: End-to-end third-party relationship risk management from vendor selection through contract termination.
**Key Business Capabilities**:
- **Third Party Catalog**: Centralized vendor inventory with risk classifications
- **Third Party Risk Management**: Vendor risk assessment, due diligence, and ongoing monitoring
- **Third Party Engagement**: Vendor lifecycle management and relationship optimization
- **Third Party Governance**: Vendor oversight, performance management, and contract compliance
- **Third Party Security Risk Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring of vendor security posture
**Integration Points**: Enterprise risk, IT security, compliance, resilience
**Maturity Progression**: Basic vendor lists → Comprehensive TPRM program → Continuous monitoring and AI insights
