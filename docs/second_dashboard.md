# Second Risk Dashboard

This document defines the properties and configuration options for a second risk dashboard. It is structured by component with clearly enumerated properties for design, implementation, and QA reference.

## Executive Summary Card Properties

- Metric Display Options: Large number format, percentage display, currency formatting
- Trend Indicators: Arrow directions, percentage changes, time period comparisons
- Status Indicators: Traffic light colors, progress bars, or gauge displays
- Alert Thresholds: Warning levels, critical levels, and acceptable ranges
- Drill-down Navigation: Click-through to detailed views or filtered reports
- Time Range Selection: Current, last 30 days, quarter, or year-to-date views
- Refresh Settings: Auto-refresh intervals, manual refresh buttons, last updated timestamps

## Risk Heat Map Properties

- Matrix Dimensions: 3x3, 4x4, 5x5, or custom sizing options
- Axis Labels: Likelihood scales (rare to almost certain), Impact scales (insignificant to catastrophic)
- Color Gradient: Heat map color schemes, intensity levels, and transparency settings
- Risk Plotting: Individual risk points, clustered risks, or density overlays
- Interactive Features: Hover tooltips, click actions, zoom capabilities
- Filter Controls: Category filters, business unit filters, time period filters
- Legend Display: Color scale legends, axis explanations, filter status indicators

## Risk Distribution Chart Properties

- Chart Types: Pie charts, donut charts, bar charts, or stacked visualizations
- Data Segmentation: By risk level, category, business unit, or ownership
- Label Display: Percentages, counts, both, or custom formatting
- Animation Effects: Loading animations, transition effects, hover animations
- Legend Positioning: Top, bottom, left, right, or floating legends
- Color Customization: Segment colors, gradients, or pattern fills
- Sorting Options: Alphabetical, by value, by risk level, or custom order

## Trend Analysis Chart Properties

- Time Series Data: Historical risk counts, assessment scores, or control effectiveness
- Chart Variations: Line graphs, area charts, column charts, or combination charts
- Time Range Controls: Zoom functionality, date pickers, preset ranges
- Multiple Data Series: Comparing different risk categories or business units
- Forecast Lines: Predictive trending, confidence intervals, scenario modeling
- Benchmark Lines: Target levels, industry averages, or regulatory thresholds
- Data Points: Markers, tooltips, crosshairs, or annotation capabilities

## Key Risk Indicator (KRI) Widgets

- Gauge Displays: Speedometer style, linear gauges, or thermometer displays
- Threshold Zones: Green (acceptable), yellow (caution), red (critical) zones
- Historical Context: Mini trend lines, sparklines, or comparison periods
- Alert Integration: Visual alerts, notification triggers, escalation rules
- Performance Metrics: Current values, target values, variance calculations
- Drill-down Details: Supporting data, calculation methods, data sources

## Risk Register Summary Properties

- Table Configuration: Column selection, sorting options, pagination settings
- Row Highlighting: Risk level colors, overdue items, recently updated risks
- Quick Actions: Edit buttons, status updates, assignment changes
- Search and Filter: Text search, advanced filters, saved filter sets
- Export Options: PDF reports, Excel exports, CSV downloads
- Bulk Operations: Mass updates, bulk assignments, batch approvals
- Column Customization: Show/hide fields, column width, reordering

## Control Effectiveness Dashboard Properties

- Assessment Status: Design effectiveness, operational effectiveness, testing status
- Control Types: Preventive, detective, corrective, or compensating controls
- Testing Schedule: Overdue tests, upcoming tests, completed assessments
- Effectiveness Ratings: Highly effective, effective, partially effective, ineffective
- Remediation Tracking: Open findings, action plan progress, completion rates
- Control Mapping: Risk coverage, control gaps, redundancy analysis

## Compliance Status Properties

- Regulatory Framework: SOX, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, or custom frameworks
- Compliance Score: Overall percentage, weighted scores, trend indicators
- Gap Analysis: Non-compliant areas, remediation timelines, resource requirements
- Audit Trail: Recent changes, approval workflows, evidence collection
- Certification Status: Current certifications, renewal dates, audit schedules
- Policy Alignment: Policy coverage, exception tracking, approval status
